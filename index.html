Updates

Autumn 2025

Just handed in my Masters thesis for the Narrative Futures program at the University of Edinburgh, which I had been doing part-time since 2022. I wrote about critical theorist Ivan Illich’s 1973 concept of conviviality and how modern computing is deteriorating it. Now recovering lost sleep. :)

I work remotely full-time as a Staff Product Designer at Monarch. Personal financial literacy is a really interesting problem space; the startup is growing rapidly and I’m constantly being challenged.

I’m also developing the “Small Hassles Court,” a playful browser-based game for conflict resolution, supported by Metagov’s Governable Spacemakers fellowship and grant. This is a subproject of Plot Twisters, my game design collective.

Finally, I’m enjoying the seasons in Cleveland, USA between road trips to New York and Cincinnati this fall. I’m organizing my closet, decorating my loft, playing soccer, and hanging out with my dog Lila and cat Cali.
